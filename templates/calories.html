FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my calorie need calculated?

It will be estimated from your age, gender, height, weight, and activity level once the calculation engine is connected.

What is a calorie deficit or surplus?

A deficit means eating fewer calories than you burn; a surplus means eating more, used for weight loss or gain respectively.

Should I recalculate often?

Recalculating every few weeks, or after a noticeable weight change, keeps the estimate accurate.
