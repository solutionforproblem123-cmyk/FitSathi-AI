Benefits

Why Follow a Workout Routine?

Regular exercise improves your physical and mental health while helping you achieve your fitness goals.

💪

Build Strength

Increase muscle strength, endurance and improve overall body performance.

❤️

Healthy Heart

Cardio workouts improve heart health and boost blood circulation.

🧠

Mental Wellness

Exercise reduces stress, improves mood and increases confidence.

Tips

Workout Tips

Warm Up

Spend 5–10 minutes warming up before every workout to reduce injury risk.

Stay Hydrated

Drink enough water before, during and after every workout session.

Recovery

Allow proper rest between workout sessions to help muscles recover.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How many days should I work out?

Beginners can start with 3 days per week. Intermediate users may train 4–5 days, while advanced users can train 5–6 days with proper recovery.

Is rest day important?

Yes. Recovery allows muscles to repair and grow, reducing the risk of injury.

Should I do cardio every day?

Light cardio can be performed regularly, but intense cardio should be balanced with strength training and adequate recovery.

Can beginners use this workout planner?

Yes. The planner is designed for beginners, intermediate and advanced users, and future backend logic will generate personalized recommendations.
