FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

BMI is a simple measure using your height and weight to estimate whether your weight is in a healthy range.

Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is a general guideline and may not fully reflect body composition for athletes or older adults.

How often should I check my BMI?

Checking every few weeks is generally enough to track meaningful changes.
