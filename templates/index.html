About FitFusion AI

Built for students, by students

FitFusion AI is a final year B.Sc. Computer Science project that combines health calculations with simple recommendation logic. The goal is to make fitness tracking approachable, while laying an architecture ready for AI-based recommendations, pose detection, and mobile conversion in the future.
