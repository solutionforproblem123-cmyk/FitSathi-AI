FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I check my progress?

Once a week is generally sufficient for tracking weight, BMI and workout consistency.

Why is consistency more important than intensity?

Consistent habits produce sustainable results over time and reduce the risk of burnout.

Will charts be available later?

Yes. Future versions will integrate Chart.js to display interactive weight, BMI and calorie history.

Can I compare my monthly progress?

Yes. Backend integration will store historical data, allowing monthly and yearly comparisons.
